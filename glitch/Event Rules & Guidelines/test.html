Event Rules & Guidelines

All attendees, speakers, sponsors and volunteers at our event are required to agree with the following code of conduct. Organizers will enforce this code throughout the event. We expect cooperation from all participants to help ensure a safe environment for everybody.

  • Be respectful of others and their points of view.
  • Refrain from demeaning, discriminatory, or harassing behavior and speech.
  • Alert event organizers if you notice a dangerous situation, someone in distress, or violations of this Code of Conduct, even if they seem inconsequential.

The event schedule is subject to change. Please check the event website for the most up-to-date schedule.

  • Registration: 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM
  • Opening keynote: 9:00 AM - 10:00 AM
  • Breakout sessions: 10:15 AM - 12:00 PM, 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM, 3:30 PM - 5:00 PM
  • Networking event: 5:30 PM - 7:30 PM

Photography and recording of any kind is prohibited during the event, except by official event photographers and videographers. By attending the event, you consent to being photographed or recorded for event-related materials.
